# AI Discoverability

AI discoverability explores how entities become visible, retrievable, and referenced within increasingly AI-native information ecosystems.

As conversational systems, retrieval engines, recommendation systems, and language models become dominant interfaces, discoverability increasingly depends on structured intelligence rather than traditional platform visibility alone.

---

## Focus Areas

- machine-readable visibility
- semantic discoverability
- retrieval optimization
- contextual relevance
- entity relationships
- structured authority
- knowledge representation
- machine-native interpretation

---

## Observation

The transition from search-first discovery toward AI-mediated interaction changes how authority, relevance, and visibility are established across digital systems.
