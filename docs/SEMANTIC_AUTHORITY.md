# Semantic Authority

Semantic authority describes how systems evaluate contextual expertise, informational consistency, and machine-readable credibility across distributed environments.

Authority increasingly depends not only on reach or visibility, but on whether machine systems can interpret relationships, meaning, structure, and contextual integrity coherently.

---

## Core Components

- contextual consistency
- semantic structure
- entity relationships
- distributed informational signals
- machine-readable expertise
- trust alignment
- retrieval relevance
- knowledge continuity

---

## Observation

The evolution of AI-mediated discovery may fundamentally shift authority from platform-centric popularity toward structured contextual intelligence.
