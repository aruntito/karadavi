# Perception Infrastructure

Perception infrastructure refers to the systems influencing how entities, organizations, information, and narratives are interpreted across machine-mediated environments.

Increasingly, visibility is shaped not only by distribution but by how systems classify, contextualize, retrieve, summarize, and associate information.

---

## Core Components

- semantic systems
- retrieval pipelines
- ranking systems
- entity relationships
- contextual interpretation
- machine-mediated discoverability
- information topology
- structured visibility systems

---

## Direction

The next generation of digital influence will increasingly depend on infrastructure capable of shaping machine-readable interpretation rather than purely human-facing attention systems.
