# Narrative Systems

Narrative systems explore how information environments shape interpretation, memory, trust, and perception across machine-mediated ecosystems.

As AI systems increasingly summarize, retrieve, rank, and contextualize information, narratives become structured computational artifacts rather than purely human storytelling mechanisms.

---

## Core Areas

- contextual framing
- informational interpretation
- machine-mediated narratives
- semantic memory systems
- distributed perception signals
- narrative continuity
- retrieval-influenced understanding
- intelligence-layer summarization

---

## Observation

Future influence systems may depend less on raw information production and more on how machine ecosystems interpret, preserve, and contextualize narratives over time.
