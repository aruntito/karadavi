# Citation Topology

Citation topology explores how references, associations, contextual mentions, and informational relationships influence machine trust and semantic authority.

In AI-native environments, visibility increasingly depends on how systems interpret interconnected information structures rather than isolated content alone.

---

## Focus Areas

- contextual references
- semantic relationships
- distributed authority signals
- information graph structures
- trust propagation
- machine-readable citations
- entity association networks
- semantic continuity

---

## Direction

The future of digital authority may increasingly emerge from structured contextual relationships distributed across interconnected information systems.
