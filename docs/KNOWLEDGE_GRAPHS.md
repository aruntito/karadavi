# Knowledge Graphs

Knowledge graphs represent structured relationship systems connecting entities, concepts, contexts, and informational signals across machine-readable environments.

They increasingly function as foundational infrastructure for retrieval systems, recommendation systems, semantic interpretation, and machine-assisted reasoning.

---

## Focus Areas

- entity relationships
- semantic mapping
- contextual associations
- graph-based intelligence
- machine-readable structures
- distributed information networks
- semantic retrieval systems
- interconnected knowledge systems

---

## Direction

As AI-native ecosystems evolve, structured relationship mapping may become one of the dominant coordination layers shaping discoverability, trust, and contextual interpretation.
